Supplemental Analysis: Persistence of Relationships
We conducted supplemental analyses to investigate the persis- tence of the effects of Day t’s customer mistreatment on rumina- tion and negative mood. To do so, we organized data into 3-day segments (i.e., t, t ϩ 1, t ϩ 2) and 4-day segments (i.e., t, t ϩ 1, t ϩ 2, t ϩ 3) to test the within-person effects of Day t’s customer mistreatment on later days’ rumination (Day t ϩ 1 and Day t ϩ 2, respectively) and morning negative mood (Day t ϩ 2 and Day t ϩ 3, respectively).
